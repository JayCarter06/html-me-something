Jeremy Carter, the Traveler

Hi, my name is Jeremy Carter, and I love to travel. I have been to over 20 states and 5 countries. Here are some of the places I have visited, and some of the places I will like to go.

Bermuda

I went to Bermuda when I was 16. I got tricked into running a half a marathon, but that is a story for another time. The scenery was unbelievable, the pure white sand, and the horizon that seemed to go on forever.

Toronto, CA

I am originally from Detroit, MI,which is minutes from the Canadian border. Therefore I found myself going back and forth quite often. I took many trips to Toronto which is still to this day my favorite big city to visit.

Jamaica Dune Falls

The first cruise I ever went on was through Royal Carribean, and the highlight of that trip was our stop in Jamaica. We had to take a hour long bus ride up a mountain into a rainforest until we reached our destination. We had arrived at the Dune Falls, which was a waterfall that you can climb up and down in groups.
